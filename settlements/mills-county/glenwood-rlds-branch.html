Glenwood RLDS Branch

MILLS CO. IAb

This RLDS Church branch could have originally been known as "Old Keg Creek" branch, located where Keg Creek flowed over the bluffs into the rich bottomlands. It was organized 1 December 1861 by Charles Derry with about 19 charter members. Many of these were formerly LDS or "Mormons" who did not continue the trek to Utah. It went through several re-organizations, the last being in 1893. The following list reflects the baptized members to about 1875.
