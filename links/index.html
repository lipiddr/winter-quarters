Mormon Overland Travel, 1847-1868
Mormon Overland Travel, 1847-1868 is the most complete database of individuals and companies in which Mormon pioneer emigrants traveled west to Utah between the years of 1847 and 1868.

Based in Omaha, Nebraska, Pioneer Research Group is working with the Winter Quarters Project to gather information about pioneer residents in the Winter Quarters area. GEDCOM files can be dowloaded here for your Personal Ancestral File projects.

The Harold B. Lee Library at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah offers thousands of research resources and links to other helpful websites.
L. Tom Perry Special Collections
The L. Tom Perry Special Collections, located at the Harold B. Lee Library at Brigham Young University, houses many original manuscripts, diaries, and other rare documents. Digital collections are also available online.
Utah Valley Regional Family History Center: BYU Family History Library
The BYU Family History Library is located in the Harold B. Lee Library and provides information about and links to family history research sources.
Trails of Hope: Overland Diaries and Letters, 1846-1869
Trails of Hope: Overland Diaries and Letters, 1846-1869 is a collection of the writings of numerous pioneer travelers. This valuable database includes original diaries, searchable trascripts, maps, trailguides, and photographs.
