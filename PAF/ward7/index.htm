Winter Quarters Ward 7 Residential Area

These pages include information about residents of the Winter Quarters 7th Ward area. The 23 Winter Quarters wards were only in existence from 1846-1848, however we have included here all of the information about the families of the ward residents up until 1853. Please note that not all of the people listed here resided at Winter Quarters.
