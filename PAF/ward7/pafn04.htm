Notes

James Madison Flake

Died of a broken neck fro being thrown from a horse.

Also lived in Winter Quarters 15th Ward.

Samuel B. Flake

Died of unknown causes and buried in grave #114.

Frederick Flake

Died on unknown causes and buried in grave #47 in Cutler's Park.

Mary Magdelena Wiekel

Came West with the Uriah Curtis Company in 1852. She then married Uriah Curtis in 1852 and lived in Springville, Utah, Utah.

Martha Ann Nichols (Nicholas)

Came West with the Edward Hunter/Jacob Foutz Company in 1847.

Found in the U. S. 1850 Census living with Sarah A. in Great Salt Lake, Utah Territory.

James Nicholas Mathis

James, Mary Catherine, and Sarah Ann came West with the Edward Hunter/Jacob Foutz Company in 1847.
