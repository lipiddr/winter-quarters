Notes

John Henry Frederick Moesser

Found on the 1860 U. S. Census of Sonora Township, Nauvoo, Illinois. Lists Elizabeth age 65 and Christopher Moisser age 24.

Mary Magdalena Zundel

Magdelena Zundel Moesser took the family West with the Daniel Spencer/Perrigrine Sessions Company in 1847. They are found on the 1850 and 1860 U. S. Census living in Salt Lake City, Utah. In 1870, Magelena, Joseph and George are living in Brighton, Salt Lake, Utah.

Gilbert Morse

Found living in Iron, Utah Territory in 1850. Listed are Gilbert age 35, Cynthia A. age 27, Mary age 7, Lidia age 3, Cynthia M. age 4 m. Traveled west in an unidentified company in 1848

Cynthia Ann Bevans

Traveled west in an unidentified company in 1848

Mary Morse

traveled west in an unidentified company in 1848

William A. Morse

Died of unknown causes and buried in grave #96.

William Amos Morse

Came West with the Brigham Young Company in 1848.

Martha Allen

Evert Ossar, age 15, was listed as coming West with the Willard Richards Company in 1848. It was probably Evert Orser.
