Chauncy Warriner Porter

The families came West in two companys. Lydia Ann Cook Porter (age 17), Alma (13), Malinda Ann (12), Nancy Aretta (4) and Warriner (infant) came wit h the Willard Richards Company in 1848. Chauncey W. (18) and Priscilla Strong Porter (18) came with the Samuel Gully/Orson Spencer Company in 1849. On the 1850 U. S. Census, they were found in the Great Salt Lake area.
