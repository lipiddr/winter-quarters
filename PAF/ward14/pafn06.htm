George Bundy

Emigrated from England on the ship Yorkshire which departed Liverpool on 8 Mar 1843. They ship arrived in New Orleans on 10 May 1843.

The family also lived in the Winter Quarters 10th Ward.

George and Mary came West with the Brigham Young Company in 1848. In the 1850 U. S. Census the family is living in Great Salt Lake, Utah Territory. In the 1860 U. S. Census, George and Mary are listed in Salt Lake City Ward 4.
