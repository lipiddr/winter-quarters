Benjamin Lynn Clapp

Benjamin, Elvira, Corinda Elvira, and Joel Henry came West with the Edward Hunter Company in 1850.

Benjamin was in charge of a company in 1856.

The family is found on the 1850 U. S. Census living in Great Salt Lake, Utah Territory. Listed are: Benjamin age 57; Mary age 36; Lewis age 18; William M. age 16; Joseph C. age 14; Mary Eliz. age 11; Margaret age 6; Sarah age 4; Nancy J. age 1. Living next door is Margaret age 77; Elvira age 26; Elvira C. age 2; and Joel age 1.

Benjamin, Elivra and family were living in Elkhorn, San Joaquin, Calfornia on the 1860 U. S. Census.

Mary and her children were living in Gilroy, Santa Clara, California.
