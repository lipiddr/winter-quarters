Notes

Francis Birch

Came West with an unidentified Company in 1850.

Elizabeth and Francis were found in the Great Salt Lake District in the Utah Territory in 1850. Ruth, age 12, was listed with them.

John Bailey

Found in the Great Salt Lake District, Utah Territory, on the 1850 Census.

On the 1880 Census John and Elizabeth are found in District 45, Salt Lake City, Utah.

Henry Banta

On the 1860 Census, Henry Banta and family was found in the Jasper township, Jasper County, Illinois.

Dorr Purdy Curtis

Came West with the William Empey Company in 1854.

Catherine Adelia Haight

Came West with the Daniel Spencer Company in 1847.
