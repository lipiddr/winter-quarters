Winter Quarters Ward 3 Residential Area

Notes

James Newberry

On the 1850 U. S. Census, James is living in District 21, Pottawattamie County, Iowa. Listed with him are Elizabeth age 35, Orson age 5, Moroni age 3, Hyrum age 2, and Heber age 1. Martha, age 18, was living with the Calvin C. Pendleton family in the same place.

James was found on the 1860 U. S. Census living in Indian Creek, Mills County, Iowa. Listed are his wife Sybil age 67, Secrotus age 15, Almasona age 13, Joseph H. age 12, Heber age 11, Joanna age 8.

James was found on the 1870 U. S. Census living in Indian Creek, Mills County, Iowa. Listed with him were Joseph age 23, Heber age 21, Joanna age 18, Charles age 25, Secratus age 25.

Martha Newberry

Came West with the Robert Wimmer Company in 1852. Only one of the family in that company.
