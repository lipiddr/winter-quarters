Joseph Grange

Listed in the IAGenWeb 1852 Census next to a ton of other Springville residents.

Harriet Loving Stanton

Kirtland, Lake, Ohio is most likely the actual birth place of Harriet because there is no Kirtland Lake, Cuyahoga, Ohio.

Clarinda Eugenia Grange

The source says that her burial was January 17, 1933 in i. o. o. f. cemetery, Wilmington, Los Angeles, California. The only problem is that I cannot find this cemetery and the only two cemeteries in Wilmington are not this one.(?)

John Barton Atchison

The Pilling and Atchison families traveled to Utah from Kanesville in a small group with four wagons.

Sarah Atchison

Sadie is also a nickname for Sarah which explains why the names on the Familysearch.org record is different.

Caroline Amelia Stanton

Evidence from census and church records prove that the Atchison family came to Utah in 1851 or 1852. Further research is needed to confirm the year they came to Utah and the name of the company they traveled with.

Pamela Atchison

The sources don't say when she died but there is no record of her in the Iowa Census for 1851. She may have passed away or her name is also Camaline or Eamaline, the child indicated as a one year old on the Iowa Census for 1851.

William West Woodland

William came to Utah in 1848.
