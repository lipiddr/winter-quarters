Joseph Mecham

Residences: Council Bluffs, Pottawattamie, Iowa, USA; 1847-1849; Baptism Date: December 25, 1845 Ordained High Priest; In 1860, Joseph had a household of 17, a real wealth of $500, and a personal wealth of $1200.
