Gideon Hayden Carter Gibbs

Came west with the Abraham O. Smoot/George B. Wallace Company 1847.

Charles Kinsey

Found living in Eureka, Humboldt, California US 1860, and in 1870 census. He is a dairyman. In 1870 he is a stockraiser. They are in the same place in the 1880 US Federal Census--now he is a farmer.

His mother and father are born in England.

Conrad Kleinman

Came West with the Brigham Young Company in 1847 and his wife came with the Abraham O. Smoot/George B. Wallace Company in 1847.
