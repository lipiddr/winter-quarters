Selected Families and Individuals

Notes

Richard Ralphs

Gone to Missouri.

Came West with the James J. Jepson Company in 1852. Ephraim and Eliza Ralphs traveled with them. These may have been children of Joseph Ralphs and Jemma Eardley. Joseph was Richard's brother.

Alfred Jason Randall

In Ohio.

Geroge Christian Riser

Shoemaker

Came West with the Abraham O. Smoot/George B. Wallace Company in 1847.

Served a mission in Germany and returned 1855 via the Seth M. Blair/Edward Stevenson Company.
