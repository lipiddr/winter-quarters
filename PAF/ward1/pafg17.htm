Joseph's family most likely called him Joel as his older brother died a few years before he was born. The name Joel appears on the census record as being 8 mon. old, which is very close to the age (9 mon) of Joseph when the census would have been taken. It also appears that the name Joel appeared in the records the Mormon Pioneer Overland Travel used to assemble their records which would explain why Joseph does not appear in any of the records with his family.
