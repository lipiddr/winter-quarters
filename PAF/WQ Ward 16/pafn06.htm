Notes

Benjamin Brown

Came West with the Brigham Young Company of 1848. They arrived in the Salt Lake Valley about 24 September 1848. Those listed in the Company are: Benjamin Brown age 53 and Sarah Mumford Brown age 53; his son Homer Brown age 17; his son Lorenzo age 25 and his wife Frances Crosby and thier son Benjamin Brown, age 3.

George W. Johnson

Listed in the Nauvoo death records. George W. Johnson died 13 Sep 1844 age 3 y 5 m 3 d of chill fever.

Mary (Polly) Miller

Came West with the Ezra T. Benson Company in 1849. Polly Miller Conklin age 57 was listed. in that company.

On the 1850 U. S. Census, Polly Conklin was living in Great Salt Lake County, Sreat Salt Lake, Utah Territory, United States. She was 59 years of age. She was living with Emily D. Partridge age 27.

In January of 1852 there was an incident that brough schism directly to the attention of the church leaders. Polly Conklin was an older widow and a Gladdenite. Polly and several other people arrived at the church offices. Polly was wanting to be sealed to John Booth. She made the mistake of defending Francis Gladden Bishop's leadership claims and doctrines. Brigham Young then suspended her from the church.

On the 1860 U. S. Census, Polly Conklin, age 68 years, is listed as living in Morrow County, Ohio with Joseph Smith, age 64, and Frances Smith, age 65.
