Notes

Elisha Averett

Elisha joined the Mormon Battalion and was a musician for Company A.

Elisha traveled with the Levi W. Hancock/Jefferson Hunt/James Pace/Andrew Lytle Company in 1847. This was a company of 117 veterans of the Battalion. After crossing the Sierra Mountains they followed a course which took them to Fort Hall and then to the Salt Lake Valley arriving between 11-16 October.

He returned to and then traveled with the George A. Smith/Dan Jones Compeny in 1849. Traveling with him were Sarah Jane With Averett age 19; Dorcas age 3 and Elisha Jr. an infant. They arrived between 26-30 October 1849.

1850 Utah Territory Census: Elisha age 40; Sarah age 20: John Witt age 22; William age 13; Dorcas age 5; Elisha age 3; Sarah age 6 mo.

On the 1860 U. S. Census, Elisha was enumerated in the 12th Ward in Great Salt Lake City, Utah. Listed are: Elisha age 49; Sarah J age 29; Dorcas age 13; Elisha age 12; Sarah J. age 9; Kesiah Ann age 7; John H. age 6; Elijah age 4; Losana age 2 and George age 2 mo.

On the 1870 U. S. Census, Elisha was enumerated in the 3rd Ward in Provo, Utah. Listed are Elisha age 59; Sarah J. age 39; Elisha age 21; John age 16; Emily age 12; George age 10; Robert age 8; James age 6; Lucy age 4; Jennette age 2.

On the 1880 U. S. Census, Elisha as enumerated in the Panguitch precinct of Iron county, Utah. Listed are Elisha age 69; Lucy S. age 13, George age 22; Robert age 20; James age 24; Jeanette age 10 and Byron P. age 8.

Death information was found on findagrave.com

Dorcas Porter Willis

Died in child birth, age 32. (Wasp 22 February 1843)
