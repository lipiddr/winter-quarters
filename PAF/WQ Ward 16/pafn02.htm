Susan Hammond

Susan married Joseph Bates Noble after her husband Nathaniel Ashby died in 1846.

She came West as Susan Hammond Ashby Noble in the Brigham Young Company of 1848. That company arrived in the Valley between 20 and 24 September 1848. Accompanying her were the following: Benjamin age 19; Harriet Maria age 15; Nathaniel age 13; Richard H. age 11; William Hardin age 9; Mary Jane age 6; Emma Smith age 5; and John Jefford age 2.

When she arrived in the Valley she was enumerated as Susan Ashby on the 1850 U. S. Census. Listed are: Susan Ashby age 43; William age 10; Mary age 8; Emma age 7; John age 6; and Louisa age 1.
