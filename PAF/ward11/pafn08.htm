Daniel Browett

While living in Nauvoo, Daniel served in the Nauvoo Legion and helped build the Nauvoo Temple.

Served in as a Seargent in Company E in the Mormon Battalion.

He was one of the three men who left a month in advance of the company to scout a road over the Sierras. They were murdered by Indians and their bodies found by the company on 19 July.
