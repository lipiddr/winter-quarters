"Lewis Abbott was b. in Wayland, Middlesex Co., Mass, Dec 18 1795, and probably m. there about 1829 , Ann Marsh, b. June 17, 1797, who was probably a dau of Thomas, of Mass. They both d. in Salt Lake, Utah, he, Aug. 26, 1851 ae. 55, and she, July 17, 1849, ae. 52 years. He was a farmer... In 1830, he joined the Mormon Ch. at the time of his death he was a high priest. He was among those who were driven from Jackson Co., Mo... After having been driven across the Missouri River into Clay Co., the following morning he recrossed the river to collect some debts, and was so beaten by a mob with a sled-stake that he never recovered from his injuries, his health being affected as long as he lived.".
