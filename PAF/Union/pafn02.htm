Nicholas Hendrickson

1. Ancestral File lists birthplace as "Connerville, Rytt, Indiana", the closest I could find to this was "Connersville, Fayette, Indiana"
2. Nicholas' continued to have children in Iowa until the year 1860. In 1865, Frances was married in Salt Lake City, Elvira in 1868, George in 1876, and Edwin in 1881, So the family probably moved west sometime between 1860 and 1865.

James Hendrickson

1. James was a private in Company "C" of the Mormon Battalion.
2. James and Martha had ten children between the years 1855 - 1875 (all but one were born in Iowa). One of them was born in 1864 in Iowa and another in 1868 was born in Wanship, Summit, Utah. But by 1870 they were back in Harrison County, Iowa for the birth of their 8th child.
So, they came west sometime between Sep 1864 and Aug 1868.
3. After the death of James, Martha joined the Reorganized Church in 1904 while living in Idaho.
